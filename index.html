First time? Once the app is open, tap the Share button (□↑) in Safari then "Add to Home Screen" — it installs like a native app and works offline on site.

How to install on iPhone

1
Open in Safari Tap the link above, or scan the QR code. Must be Safari — not Chrome.
2
Tap Share □↑ The share button is at the bottom of the screen in Safari.
3
Add to Home Screen Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Done — works offline Open from your home screen like any app. All data saves locally on your phone.

How to install on Android

1
Open in Chrome Tap the link or scan the QR code. Chrome works best on Android.
2
Tap ⋮ Menu Three dots in the top right corner of Chrome.
3
Add to Home Screen Tap "Add to Home screen" and confirm. The app icon appears immediately.
